# product-management-case-studies
Building product thinking in public through 90 real-world case studies across Healthcare, AI, SaaS, and Consumer products. Documenting user research, product strategy, UX, metrics, and PM frameworks while building Aaroh.
Hi, I'm Gaurav.

I'm transitioning into Product Management after working in Healthcare Research, Psychology, and Integrative Medicine. Through my experience, I became fascinated by how great products solve meaningful problems and create real impact for users.

Instead of only learning through courses, I've decided to learn by analyzing real products.

This repository documents that journey.

---

# 🎯 The Challenge

**90 Days. 90 Product Case Studies.**

Every day, I'll analyze one product, understand the users behind it, evaluate business decisions, and think through improvements from a Product Manager's perspective.

This isn't about finding the "right" answer.

It's about improving my product thinking through consistent practice.

---

# 📚 What You'll Find Here

Each case study may include:

- Problem Statement
- User Research
- User Personas
- Pain Points
- User Journey
- Product Strategy
- Business Model
- Growth Strategy
- Product Metrics
- Competitive Analysis
- UX Observations
- Opportunities for Improvement
- PM Recommendations

Not every case study will follow the exact same framework. As I learn, my approach will continue to evolve.
